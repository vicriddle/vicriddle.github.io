ABOUT VICTORIA

Victoria is a Library Technician with a multi-faceted skill set in virtual resources, database and e-reserves management. She has a Library and Information Technician Diploma from Seneca College, a Bachelor of Social Work degree from Toronto Metropolitan University (formerly Ryerson University) and is currently pursuing a Master in Library Science at Western University part-time. Eager to build on her academic foundations, she considers herself as a 'forever student' pursuing knowledge through coursework and professional development within the Library Community.


Her work experience is vast, spanning across multiple disciplines including Administration, Libraries, Social Work, Retail, and Food Services. She loves spending time working with students in promote reading and literacy, as well as explore new technology in library spaces with STEAM activities. SHe is passionate about environmentalism, social justice and her community, volunteering across agencies such as Kids Help Phone and Big Brother, Big Sister and is a fierce advocate for mental health supports.
